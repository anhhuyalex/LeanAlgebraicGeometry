11.1 Source citations for the four (S3.*) sub-claims

The four (S3.*) sub-claims are stated and proved in RigidityKbar.tex §"Chart-algebra piece (ii) first-class decomposition" (175, 176, 177, 178); we do not duplicate those statements here. This section records, for the per-Lean-file pointer chapter, the verbatim Stacks-source citations backing each sub-claim, copied character-for-character from the in-tree bundled chapter sources (the basis of the iter-151 tag corrections above).

(S3.sep.1) Smooth \(\Rightarrow \) \(\Gamma \) geometrically reduced.

Source: Stacks Tags 035U and 056T. A smooth morphism \(X \to \operatorname{Spec}k\) has geometrically reduced source; combined with the flat-base-change identification (S3.pi.1) this gives geometric reducedness of \(\Gamma (X, \mathcal O_X)\) as a \(k\)-algebra. Canonical statement: 175. DESCOPED under the alg-closed pivot — this sub-claim is the general-over-\(k\) formulation and is NO LONGER on the M2.a critical path (constants_integral_over_base_field now closes over \(\bar k\) via IsAlgClosed.algebraMap_bijective_of_isIntegral); retained as an auditable / upstream-Mathlib-PR record.

(S3.sep.2) Geometrically reduced finite extension \(\Rightarrow \) separable.

Source: Stacks Tag 0BUG, part (4). Part (4) packages exactly "geometrically reduced finite-over-\(k\) factor \(\Rightarrow \) finite separable over \(k\)", the statement abstracted to a standalone finite-field-extension lemma here. Canonical statement: 176. DESCOPED under the alg-closed pivot — this sub-claim is the general-over-\(k\) formulation and is NO LONGER on the M2.a critical path (constants_integral_over_base_field now closes over \(\bar k\) via IsAlgClosed.algebraMap_bijective_of_isIntegral); retained as an auditable / upstream-Mathlib-PR record.

(S3.pi.1) Flat base change of \(\Gamma \) for proper schemes.

Source: Stacks Tag 02KH, part (2). Specialising part (2) to \(\mathcal F = \mathcal O_X\), \(i = 0\), \(A = k\), \(B = K\) gives the \(K\)-algebra isomorphism \(\Gamma (X, \mathcal O_X) \otimes _k K \cong \Gamma (X_K, \mathcal O_{X_K})\). Canonical statement: 177. DESCOPED under the alg-closed pivot — this sub-claim is the general-over-\(k\) formulation and is NO LONGER on the M2.a critical path (constants_integral_over_base_field now closes over \(\bar k\) via IsAlgClosed.algebraMap_bijective_of_isIntegral); retained as an auditable / upstream-Mathlib-PR record.

(S3.pi.2) Unique-minimal-prime base change \(\Rightarrow \) purely inseparable.

Source: Stacks Tags 09HD and 030K (Lemma 9.14.6). A finite extension \(F/k\) whose base change \(F \otimes _k \bar k\) has a unique minimal prime is local-after-\(\otimes \bar k\), forcing trivial separable part in the 030K factorisation, hence \(F/k\) purely inseparable. Canonical statement: 178. DESCOPED under the alg-closed pivot — this sub-claim is the general-over-\(k\) formulation and is NO LONGER on the M2.a critical path (constants_integral_over_base_field now closes over \(\bar k\) via IsAlgClosed.algebraMap_bijective_of_isIntegral); retained as an auditable / upstream-Mathlib-PR record.
