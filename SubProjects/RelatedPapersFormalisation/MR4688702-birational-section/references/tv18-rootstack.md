## Caveats
- The directive listed arXiv:1412.7523 as the seed for this paper; that ID belongs to a completely different paper (classical mechanics). The correct ID is **1410.1164**.
- The infinite root stack `∞√X` is defined in Definition 3.3 (p. 13) as the root stack `√(AQ)X` where `AQ` denotes the rationalization of the sheaf `A`. The projective system presentation `lim_n √n X` appears in Proposition 3.5 (p. 14).
