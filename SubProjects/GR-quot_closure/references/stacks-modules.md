## Why this source
Backs the new blueprint chapter `Picard_SectionGradedRing.tex` building infrastructure for the section
graded ring R(X_s,L_s) = ⊕_m Γ(X_s, L_s^{⊗m}) and graded module M = ⊕_m Γ(X_s, F_s ⊗ L_s^{⊗m}).
Needed for VERBATIM definition blocks: (a) tensor product of sheaves of modules as sheafification of
the pointwise presheaf, and (b) tensor power + Γ_* graded ring of an invertible sheaf.
