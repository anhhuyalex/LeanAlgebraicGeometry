## Structural problem (abstracted)
We have two adjunctions `adj₀₁ : F₀₁ ⊣ G₁₀`, `adj₁₂ : F₁₂ ⊣ G₂₁` whose right adjoints `Gᵢ` are
lax-monoidal (so the left adjoints `Fᵢ` carry the canonical *oplax* structure, δ = mate of μ). We
also have a single adjunction `adj₀₂ : F₀₂ ⊣ G₂₀` and a **monoidal** natural iso of the right
adjoints `e₀₁₂ : G₂₁ ⋙ G₁₀ ≅ G₂₀` (here `pushforwardComp`). This induces the left-adjoint
composition iso `c := leftAdjointCompIso adj₀₁ adj₁₂ adj₀₂ e₀₁₂ : F₀₁ ⋙ F₁₂ ≅ F₀₂` (here
`pullbackComp`). The structural claim ("Sq2b") is that **`c` is a monoidal natural iso** between the
oplax functors `(F₀₁⋙F₁₂, comp_δ)` and `(F₀₂, leftAdjointOplaxMonoidal)`:
```
δ(F₀₂) M N = c.inv.app (M⊗N) ≫ δ(F₀₁⋙F₁₂) M N ≫ (c.hom.app M ⊗ₘ c.hom.app N)
```
where `δ(F₀₁⋙F₁₂) = comp_δ = F₁₂.map (δ F₀₁ M N) ≫ δ F₁₂ _ _`.

## Analogues found

### Analogue: `CategoryTheory.Adjunction.leftAdjointOplaxMonoidal` + `leftAdjointOplaxMonoidal_δ` (`Mathlib/CategoryTheory/Monoidal/Functor.lean:1009`, simp lemma at :1063)
- **Domain**: category theory — monoidal/adjunction mate calculus (the project's own area, but the
  *abstract* layer, one shelf up from `PresheafOfModules`).
- **Same structural problem there**: defines the oplax δ of a left adjoint as the **mate** of the
  lax μ of its right adjoint: `δ F X Y := (adj.homEquiv _ _).symm ((unit X ⊗ₘ unit Y) ≫ μ G X Y)`.
  Hence `homEquiv (δ F X Y) = (unit X ⊗ₘ unit Y) ≫ μ G X Y` — the canonical bridge that converts any
  δ-equation into a μ-equation on the right-adjoint side.
- **Technique**: `apply (adj.homEquiv _ _).injective`, then rewrite the LHS δ with
  `leftAdjointOplaxMonoidal_δ` (+ `Equiv.apply_symm_apply`) to land on `(unit⊗unit)≫μ`.
- **Mapping to project**: the project's `presheafPullbackOplaxMonoidal` (TensorObjSubstrate.lean:1146)
  IS `leftAdjointOplaxMonoidal` of `PresheafOfModules.pullbackPushforwardAdjunction φ'`. So
  `δ (PresheafOfModules.pullback φ')` is exactly this Mathlib δ, and `leftAdjointOplaxMonoidal_δ` is
  the bridge — no project-specific `…homEquiv_pullbackObjUnitToUnit` lemma is needed (that one
  existed only because `pullbackObjUnitToUnit` was a project def; `δ` is a Mathlib def).
- **Porting cost**: low. The bridge is already in Mathlib.
- **Verdict**: ANALOGUE_FOUND.

### Analogue: `CategoryTheory.Adjunction.conjugateEquiv_leftAdjointCompIso_inv` (`Mathlib/CategoryTheory/Adjunction/CompositionIso.lean:82`)
- **Domain**: category theory — mate calculus for composition coherences of adjoint functors.
- **Same structural problem there**: `leftAdjointCompIso adj₀₁ adj₁₂ adj₀₂ e₀₁₂` (= the abstract form
  of `pullbackComp`) is **defined** as the conjugate/mate of `e₀₁₂` (= `pushforwardComp`):
  `conjugateEquiv (adj₀₁.comp adj₁₂) adj₀₂ (leftAdjointCompIso …).inv = e₀₁₂.hom`. This is the exact
  bridge that turns `pullbackComp.inv/.hom` into `pushforwardComp.hom/.inv` under transpose.
- **Technique**: the project already uses this — `Scheme.Modules.conjugateEquiv_pullbackComp_inv`
  (Sheaf.lean:238) is its specialization, consumed in the COMPILING `pullbackObjUnitToUnit_comp`
  (TensorObjSubstrate.lean:921). The presheaf-level form is `PresheafOfModules.pullbackComp` is
  `leftAdjointCompIso(…)` directly, so `conjugateEquiv_leftAdjointCompIso_inv` applies verbatim.
- **Mapping to project**: in Sq2b's transposed (μ-side) form, `c.inv`/`c.hom` become
  `pushforwardComp.hom`/`.inv`; the goal reduces to the **lax μ-composition-coherence of
  `PresheafOfModules.pushforward` across `pushforwardComp`** — which is concrete/sectionwise (cf.
  `pushforwardComp_hom_app_app = 𝟙` rfl, Sheaf.lean:214; and the η-analog
  `unitToPushforwardObjUnit_comp` closes by `rfl`, TensorObjSubstrate.lean:881).
- **Porting cost**: low. Bridge in Mathlib; project specialization already proven & used.
- **Verdict**: ANALOGUE_FOUND.

### Analogue: `CategoryTheory.Adjunction.isMonoidal_comp` (`Mathlib/CategoryTheory/Monoidal/Functor.lean:990`)
- **Domain**: category theory — the canonical "composite of monoidal adjunctions is monoidal".
- **Same structural problem there**: proves `(adj.comp adj').IsMonoidal`, whose `leftAdjoint_μ` field
  is precisely a δ/μ-transport equation, discharged by the mate bookkeeping
  `comp_δ` + `δ_natural_assoc` + `← tensorHom_comp_tensorHom` + `comp_unit_app`/`comp_counit_app`.
- **Technique**: `simp only [comp_μ, IsMonoidal.leftAdjoint_μ (adj := adj), …, comp_δ,
  ← tensorHom_comp_tensorHom, δ_natural_assoc, Functor.comp_map]; simp`.
- **Mapping to project**: this is the *bookkeeping template* for the tensor-of-two-arguments steps in
  Sq2b (the `tensorHom`/`δ_natural` shuffling that the η-analog `pullbackObjUnitToUnit_comp` did not
  need). BUT it does **not close Sq2b wholesale**: `isMonoidal_comp` is about the COMPOSITE adjunction
  `adj.comp adj'` (left adjoint `F⋙F'`), whereas Sq2b transports across the ISO `c = pullbackComp` to
  the *single* functor/adjunction `F₀₂ / adj₀₂`. The cross-iso transport is still needed (analogues 1+2).
- **Porting cost**: medium (as a sub-routine, not a one-shot).
- **Verdict**: PARTIAL_ANALOGUE.

## Top suggestion
Prove Sq2b **at the `PresheafOfModules` level** as a standalone lemma — NOT at the Scheme/`forget₂`
level — by mirroring the COMPILING `pullbackObjUnitToUnit_comp` with η→δ. State it about
`PresheafOfModules.pullbackComp φ'_f φ'_h : pullback φ'_f ⋙ pullback φ'_h ≅ pullback (φ'_f ≫
whiskerLeft F.op φ'_h)` and `δ` of `pullback (φ'_f ≫ whiskerLeft …)` (NOT bare `pullback
(toRingCatSheafHom (h≫f)).hom`) — this makes the `(F:=…⋙…)`/associativity pinning come from
`pullbackComp`'s own signature, dissolving iter-257 friction (2); reconcile to `pullback φ'_{h≫f}`
only at the end via finding (1)'s `rfl` (`toRingCatSheafHom_comp_hom_reconcile`). Working at presheaf
level dissolves friction (1) (the `forget₂` instance is fixed by `presheafPullbackOplaxMonoidal`'s
`φ' : (S₀⋙forget₂) ⟶ F.op⋙(R₀⋙forget₂)` spelling, exactly as `pullbackTensorMap` already binds `φ'`).
Proof skeleton: `apply (PresheafOfModules.pullbackPushforwardAdjunction φ'_{comp}).homEquiv _ _
|>.injective`; rewrite LHS δ via `leftAdjointOplaxMonoidal_δ`; transpose RHS via
`conjugateEquiv_pullbackComp_inv` (presheaf form = `Adjunction.conjugateEquiv_leftAdjointCompIso_inv`)
+ `unit_conjugateEquiv` + `Adjunction.comp_unit_app`; the residual is the lax-μ coherence of
`PresheafOfModules.pushforward` across `pushforwardComp`, sectionwise/`rfl` (cf.
`unitToPushforwardObjUnit_comp` and `pushforwardComp_hom_app_app = 𝟙`). Borrow the two-argument
`tensorHom`/`δ_natural_assoc`/`← tensorHom_comp_tensorHom` shuffle line-for-line from
`Adjunction.isMonoidal_comp` (Functor.lean:995-999).
